Project O.N.E.

Carrying Hopes in a Caravan


The lifespan of Homo Sapiens is marked by the successive change of events beginning from gestation and ending in death. This cycle of life is completely inevitable. Forgetting this paramount message of life, the youth of our country is inclined towards treating their parents as a liability. Instead of preserving their parents as a valuable treasure, children often lead them to the doors of old age homes. Moreover, due to increasing population and Darwin’s Theory of “Survival of the fittest”, infants are left to die on the streets of social and mental abuse. More than 4% of our country lies in the orphanages and suffer the cruelty of life.
Project O.N.E. takes an initiative to meet these emotionally deprived grandparents and fellow beautiful creatures of God. The drive aims to cover the various states of our country. We wish to visit an old age home or an orphanage for a day and make it really special for them. We will travel these distances living a van life that means wherever we park, we make it our home. Conducting various fun games and activities, having tasty meals with them, listening to their stories and living their dreams, we might end up giving some direction to their lives.
These old and new earthlings need affection, attention, and edification in order to live a happy life. Hence we call it ONE, which stands for Old and New Earthlings.

Our Approach


India, being the second most populous country, comprises of about 1.252 billion people. Our country has around 100 million elderly at present and the number is expected to increase by multiple folds. But sadly, out of every 10 elderly couples, more than 6 are forced to leave their homes by their offsprings. These emotionally destitute parents finally find some shelter in the old age homes. Additionally, 41% of the country constitutes of the population below 18 years of age. Approximately 20 million children are orphans. These completely helpless souls grow up in tough situations bearing all kinds of sexual, mental, physical and emotional abuse.
Keeping these major social issues in our hearts, we have come up with the Project O.N.E (Old and New Earthlings). The aim of the drive is to spread the wave of love and affection all across the country. Through our 45 days long van life we would sprinkle the happiness and warmth across the various Old Age Homes and Orphanages. This would include various fun filled activities like
Roller-skating and skateboarding
Painting
Projecting movies
Photography
Planting trees
And much more..
Through this project, we wish to spread out the message that how a single well spent day can hold everlasting significance for them. This journey will also inspire people to live the van life and travel, leaving behind their mundane lives.
Our caravan will start from Delhi. We will buy a van and transform it into our rolling home. The journey will then head towards Rajasthan, Gujrat, Maharashtra, Goa, Karnataka, Telangana, Madhya Pradesh and return back to Delhi via Uttar Pradesh. In Project ONE where we bring together our desire to wander, go distances, meet new people, connect with them and plant in them the seed of happiness. We will shoot our journey on a video camera and we aspire to make a documentary that would assist us in reaching out to more number of people and thus creating a vast impact.
